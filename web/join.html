Join Us in Developing VoteTorrent

VoteTorrent is a work in progress, and we need your help to bring it to fruition. We invite developers, testers, and anyone interested in secure and fair elections to join our community. By contributing your skills and feedback, you can help shape the future of voting.

Here are some ways you can get involved:

  • Coding: Contribute to the development of VoteTorrent by writing and reviewing code. Check out our GitHub repository for issues and feature requests.
  • Testing: Help us test VoteTorrent to ensure it is secure, reliable, and user-friendly. Your feedback is crucial in identifying and fixing bugs.
  • Providing Feedback: Share your thoughts and suggestions on how we can improve VoteTorrent. Your input helps us make the system better for everyone.
  • Promoting the Project: Spread the word about VoteTorrent to your community and network. The more people involved, the stronger and more effective the system will be.

Visit our GitHub Repository to get started. Join our mailing list and participate in discussions to support the development of VoteTorrent. Together, we can create a secure and trustworthy voting system for the future.
